• Prepaid Encryption Plans

    Encryption plans are pay-as-you-go and are required for encrypting and sharing encrypted data.

    There are no fees for decrypting data. Encryption plans can be purchased through any Venux product and never expire.

    The size of the encrypted data is deducted from the amount of encryption you have left. For example, if you encrypt a 1GB file with a 10GB encryption plan, you will be left with 9GB of encryption.

    Please contact us for custom plans over 100GB.
